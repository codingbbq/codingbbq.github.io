## What Went Well
- It was around November - December 2019 that I decided I would go flutter full swing. I loved the output I could see. Considering it is Object orientend dart, I got to understand and learn new paradigms which was a plus. I would watch all the flutter tutorials on youtube and follow along to improve my understanding. It was satisfying.
- Around January 2020, I decided to do flutter challenges. The idea was to develop designs from Dribble and elsewhere into functioning prototypes.
- I now understand the power of getting started. Sometimes it is daunting but once you develop something, the output is worth every second of efforts you put into it. Now my approach is to just get my hands dirty.
- Sometimes during development, I found myself stuck at a problem. It was very fortunate to find solutions online on platforms like stackoverflow and discord etc. It is amazing to see that a community of flutter developers are always around to help and provide inputs. I realized that flutter is not just about creating new apps but also about making tutorials, providing solutions on stackoverflow etc. 
- I have decided to log every detail related to technology, flutter that I find interesting and that I would want to come back in future and refer to myself. So all the blog posts that are on codingbbq are my thoughts decluttered and available for future references, for me. I would considering it a blessing if even one post is useful to anyone facing any challenges.
- I completed #03 Flutter challenges and in each challenge, I found myself evolved. 

## What did not go well
- I did not push myself. During the time when I quit something, It looked it was'nt necessary. However, when I write it down analyzing the 31 days of my work, I feel I should be pushing myself harder. I hope to achieve mental satisfaction in coming months.
- My schedule have been hectic beccause of the long commute hours, because of which I have almost quit my habit of excercising. I plead guilty.
- I did not write as much as I had planned for.
- I had decided to do atleast one commit a day on my codingbbq github account. I think I failed on several occasions.
