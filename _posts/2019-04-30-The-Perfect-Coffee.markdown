Coffee, to me, is not how it tastes, but how it makes me feel. I have read numerous articles as to why one should leave coffee and what are the bad effects of drinking coffee, but this essay is not about that. Mind you, the irony is, I am writing this post while I sip my black tea. I've already had my stress buster, aromatic, addictive black coffee right when I felt I needed one. It made me feel relaxed. 

Growing up, I have always heard how a cup of coffee is equivalent to two cigarettes. For what it's worth, I have no intention of becoming a sherlock to hunt the truth. Certain habits just build up and become a daily routine to the sub-conscious mind. For me, drinking coffee is one of them. But not just any cup of coffee, it has to be the P-E-R-F-E-C-T cup. 

### The perfect coffee?

Yes, The Perfect Cup of Coffee. It may sound as stupid as it may be, I am sure there is atleast one internet nerd who resonate with my thoughts. So, for me, The perfect coffee is the one that I prepare myself. Yes, the coffee machine does the hard work, where I stand still, absorbing the husss... and the hiss.. and the hubbub at the vicinity, I enjoy the effortless thoughts that run by me during that course of time. It is my time. 

When at work, I make sure I press the esspresso button and when it pours half of the aromatic coffee, I press it again. I don't want the strongest of the coffee. I then dilute it with two presses of hot water. Done. Happy as I can be, I am ready to sip every drop of my coffee. 

Now that the coffee is ready, don't you wish to know where I would love to drink it? I go to the topmost floor of the building, the last staircase, which hardly anyone even considers going to, sit with my headpones and listen to the music of Linkin park while I enjoy every drop of the coffee.

When at home, I microwave a cup full of water exactly at one minute and thirty seconds. Followed by half lemon for that pinchy taste and then a tiny spoon of nescafe. Lemon gives a kick to the taste.

A cup of coffee keeps me away from all the cluttered thoughts. I have my own virtual space and my own time for a crystal clear flow of information. I enjoy that time.

### Where your coffee maker, dude?

As much as I would love to have one, I don't think I am missing a coffee maker. Having watched countless videos on youtube about how to brew a coffee, and mind you, I surely will one day brew my own, but for now, I am super happy with the routine I have. 


### Tiny little things make me happy

So, even though I've had my coffee and now that I am sipping my black tea, It is obvious that I love some kind of beverage. A black coffee makes me super enthusiastic and happy and at the same time raises my energy levels. If it sounds vague to anyone reading this, I may have failed at my words or you have never experianced the tiny nuggets of happiness in those little things in your life.

So what you waiting for, spend that me time with yourself which makes you feel gigantic within, no matter how minutely unnoticeable it is to the world.
