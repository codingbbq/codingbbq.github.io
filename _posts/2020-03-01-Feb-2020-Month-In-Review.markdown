Febraury has been a good month in terms of Job, as I did achieve quite a bit of atomic tasks. Sometimes it is the little things that add up and I can feel them. Anyways this blog post is not about my Job, it is about my personal productivity. I had set (What looked like) an achievable goal for myself.  Below is my retrospection.

## What Went Well
- I had set myself a target of 2 Flutter UI challenges, I got just one done. However, the best part was I did achieve the functionality that I had planned for.
- Working on the Trello board for the remember me app was a good idea. I plan to continue doing so forever.
- I created 3 youtube vides, nothing exotic, just screen casts of all the Flutter UI challenges showing a walk through of what I could achieve. They say, picture speaks a thousand words, I say videos should a million words. 

## What did not go well
- I could not get that 2nd Flutter app done. In short I achieved just 50% of what I had planned for February. 
- I spent a lot of time figuring out what I wanted to do for the next challenge. I think I should not be choosy abouut the designs I pick to work on. There are so many designs out there on Dribbble, Behance and pinterest that I get lost and fail to come to a conclusion. I think I spent almost 5-6 days just browsing through the designs and not even selecting one of it. 

## Planning for March 2020
- Found this simple design on dribbble which I want to take up as the next challenge. Also, I do not want to set high expectations this month.
- One. Complete at least two Flutter UI challenges along with youtube videos.
- Two. Follow the freecodecamp tutorial on React and Firebase. It is 12 hrs of video.
